Who We Are

At Thiru M.S.Ramaiah Nadar Memorial Bose Nursery and Primary School, We and our dedicated educators provide quality education since 2001. We foster holistic development in a nurturing environment through Thiru R.BOSE-VASUKI Educational Trust.

What We Do

Thiru M.S.Ramaiah Nadar Memorial Bose Nursery and Primary School offers a Mindfull curriculum blending academic excellence and extracurricular activities. Our aim is attaining the excellence through education. We have the courses from prekg, LKG, UKG and class I to V.

Why Choose Us

Thiru M.S.Ramaiah Nadar Memorial Bose Nursery and Primary School assures an unparalleled educational experience. We offer expertise and individualized attention at affordable fees. Along with the curriculum we do give Hindi, Computer Science, Karate, Yoga, Dance and Arts at no additional cost.
